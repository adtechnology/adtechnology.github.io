Marketing in the 21st century is rapidly changing as new technologies affect how people get information and make decisions. As a marketing professional, I have embraced these changes, using inventions such as mobile devices and tablets to my advantage.

I currently work at Axel Springer Media Impact, one of the largest European digital publishing houses that hosts many multimedia news brands. The company spans over 40 countries and is involved in projects ranging from responsive website design to advertising across multiple technologies. As a team leader in the Ad Technology department, I am responsible for implementing technological solutions for efficient sales.

Earlier in my career at Axel Springer, I worked as a manager for the Mobile Applications and Tablets division. As I worked to develop cost-effective strategies for mobile sales, I gained a deep knowledge of mobile technology, applications, and systems architecture. I have been able to combine this knowledge of technology with my marketing expertise in order to create efficient, practical solutions to marketing challenges.

Prior to my employment at Axel Springer, however, I had already begun to learn how to collaborate on projects that broke down the boundaries between marketing and technology. When I moved from Ecuador to Germany to get a master's degree, I became the head of quality assurance at Softgames Mobile Entertainment Services. Even as I honed my leadership skills, I successfully collaborated with designers, programmers, and other project managers.

Throughout my career I have been assisted by my ability to learn, and continue to look for opportunities to deepen my knowledge. This includes knowledge of other cultures; I love to travel, and take annual trips to the US. I have used my knowledge of people and business to develop a unique management style that allows for successful, efficient completion of projects.
